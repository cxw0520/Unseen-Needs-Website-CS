What is Visible?

Needs that are often easy to recognize from appearance alone.

Caregiving

Caregivers with children are easy to recognize. Their needs are visible, and we often respond.

Wheelchair Use

People who use wheelchairs are often easy to recognize. Their needs are visible, and we can respond by giving them space.

Aging

Older adults are often easy to recognize. Their needs are visible, and we often offer them seats or assistance.

What is
Invisible?

Invisible needs are often difficult to recognize from appearance alone.

They can include anxiety, sensory sensitivity, chronic pain, fatigue, mental health conditions, reduced stamina, or temporary physical discomfort.
