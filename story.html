STORY ABOUT US

The Genesis of Empathy

UNSEEN was born from a simple, profound realization: the vast majority of human struggle happens beneath the surface. We move through shared spaces carrying invisible burdens—chronic pain, anxiety, neurodivergence—often feeling entirely alone in a crowd.

Our mission is not merely to acknowledge these unseen truths, but to make them tangible. To weave a new visual language of radical empathy.
