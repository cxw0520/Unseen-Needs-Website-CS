# Unseen Needs Design System (VI)

This document outlines the Visual Identity (VI) for the Unseen Needs project, as defined in the official design documentation.

## 1. Brand Identity
### Logo
The logo features the text **"UNSE  N"**. The deliberate gap between 'E' and 'N' symbolizes the "unseen" aspect of various needs and conditions.

## 2. Typography
The project utilizes a combination of serif and sans-serif fonts to balance approachability with clarity.

- **Primary Serif:** Tobias
  - Variants: Regular, Light
  - Usage: Headings, emphasis, and primary storytelling text.
- **Primary Sans-Serif:** Franklin Gothic Medium
  - Variants: Regular
  - Usage: Body text, UI elements, and technical descriptions.

## 3. Color Palette
The palette consists of vibrant yet soft tones used to categorize different types of needs and provide visual feedback.

| Color | Hex Code | Usage Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Blue** | `#4680BE` | Energy & Endurance |
| **Red** | `#D74737` | Critical / Attention |
| **Pink** | `#EBB5CA` | Physical Support |
| **Yellow** | `#E5C543` | Emotional / Cognitive |
| **Teal** | `#68AC9D` | General Elements |
| **Purple** | `#6D65A1` | General Elements |
| **Dark Brown** | `#4B332D` | Text / Branding |

## 4. Visual Elements
### Iconography
Needs are represented by stylized, geometric human figures constructed from basic shapes (circles, triangles).

#### Invisible Needs

## 5. Core Messaging & Themes
The design system categorizes support into three main themes, each with specific color coding and messaging:

### 🟡 Emotional / Cognitive
- **Color:** Yellow (`#E5C543`)
- **Key Message:** "I may need space. I may need quiet. I'm feeling overwhelmed. Please be gentle with me."

### 💗 Physical Support
- **Color:** Pink (`#EBB5CA`)
- **Key Message:** "I may need a seat. I may need assistance. I'm managing a physical condition. Please be considerate with me."

### 🔵 Energy Endurance
- **Color:** Blue (`#4680BE`)
- **Key Message:** "I may need to rest. I may move slowly. I'm easily fatigued. Please be patient with me."
